Standard Issue Gear Checklist

What You Get

  • Standard Starfleet Uniform (Red)

    Highly visible to hostiles

  • Type-2 Phaser

    Usually set to stun, often ineffective

  • Communicator

    Works 60% of the time

  • Tricorder

    If your lucky and science is busy

What You Don't Get

  • Body Armor

    Deemed unnecessary by Starfleet Command

  • Medical Supplies

    Dr. McCoy handles that

  • Extra Power Cells

    Budget constraints

  • Backup weapon

    One phaser is considered adequate

  • Survival kit

    You won't need it...probably
