Hall of Shame: Epic Redshirt Fails

  • "They went boldy where no one should have gone..."

    • Ensign Ricky, Stardate 3045.6
    • Eipsode: "The Apple"
    • Incident: Steps on explosive rocks
    • Lesson Learned: Watch your step!
    • Mister Hendorff, Stardate 3715.3
    • Episode: "The Apple"
    • Incident: First to die while exploring paradise-jungle killed by poisonous plant.
    • Lesson Learned: Sometimes you don't even get a chance.
    • Ensign Wyatt, Stardate Unnkown
    • Episode: "That Which Survives"
    • Incident: Killed by alien hologram in transporter room.
    • Lesson Learned: The Transporter room isn't safer than the Bridge.
    • Lt. Galloway, Stardate Unknown
    • Episode: "The Omega Glory"
    • Incident: Vaporized by phaser blast at the hands of Captain Ronald Tracey.
    • Lesson Learned: Never trust a Starfleet Captain who isn't your own.
    • Ensign Rizzo, Stardate 3619.2
    • Episode: "Obession"
    • Incident: Drained of blood by gaseous vampire cloud
    • Lesson Learned: If it looks like mist, run!

  • "May their sacrifices never be forgotten, and their mistakes never be repeated."
