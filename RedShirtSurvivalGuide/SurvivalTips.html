Past Notes from Pervious Security Officers
DO
  • Stay at least 3 meters behind the Captain at all times
  • Voluenteer for bridge duty instead of away missions whenever possible
  • Keep your phaser on stun (you probably won't get to use it anyways)
  • Memorize the location of the nearest rock to hide behind
  • If you hear dramatic music, request an immediate beam-up
  • Always check if the planet has a suspiciously Earth-like atmosphere
  • Buddy system with science officers - they have better survival rates
DONT
  • Never be the first to investigate a strange noise
  • Don't touch alien atrifacts without a science officer present
  • Avoid splitting up from the landing party
  • Never say 'What could possibly go wrong?'
  • Don't approach beautiful alien women alone
  • Refrain from examining suspicious spores or plants
  • Don't wander off to check the perimeter by yourself
  • Never volunteer to guard the pirsoner
